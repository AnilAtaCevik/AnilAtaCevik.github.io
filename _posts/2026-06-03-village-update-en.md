## Non-Player Characters (NPCs)

To make the village feel alive and functional, the core merchant NPCs have been integrated into the map:

### Blacksmith
The local forge where players can spend their gold to purchase new weapons and armor, or sell their old gear.
![Blacksmith](/assets/images/Blacksmith.webp)

### Wizard
A mystical vendor specializing in accessories. Here, players can buy and sell various rings and pendants for gold.
![Wizard](/assets/images/Wizard.webp)

### Alchemist
A vital stop for survival, providing an assortment of potions available for purchase and sale using gold.
![Alchemist](/assets/images/Alchemist.webp)

### Merchant
The economic center for adventurers looking to offload rare and valuable items in exchange for large sums of gold.
![Merchant](/assets/images/Merchant.webp)

## Key Districts & Zones

The village is divided into distinct functional zones that prepare the player for their journey:

### Bar
A lively tavern environment. In future updates, this location will be populated with NPCs offering various story-driven quests.
![Bar](/assets/images/Bar.webp)

### MissionBoard
A dedicated spot for side activities. It provides several alternative missions that contribute to player progression without impacting the main narrative.
![MissionBoard](/assets/images/MissionBoard.webp)

### PlayerZone
A personal space for the player. It features a mirror for altering the character's visual appearance and a storage chest to safely stash away excess inventory items.
![PlayerZone](/assets/images/PlayerZone.webp)

### CaveEntrance
The threshold to the unknown. This pathway grants direct access to the cave systems where the core action of the game will take place.
![CaveEntrance](/assets/images/CaveEntrance.webp)
